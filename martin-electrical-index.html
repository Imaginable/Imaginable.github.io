The HERO Program is now in Los Angeles & Riverside Counties!

HERO stands for Home Enery Renovation Opportunity. This finance option works with local governments to make energy efficient, water efficient, and renewable energy products more affordable for home owners. With felxible playments added to your property taxes, and low fixed interest rates, this program is perfect for anyone. Martin's Elctrical is a contractor under the HERO program and can help you upgrade your home today with this wonderful opportunity.

To find out if the HERO program is available in your county, or if you would like to apply for HERO financing; pleace visit the hero program website or call 1-855-HERO-411.
